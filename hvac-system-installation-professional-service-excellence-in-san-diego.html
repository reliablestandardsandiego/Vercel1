The Critical Importance of Professional Installation


Why Installation Quality Matters

Even the highest-efficiency HVAC equipment will underperform without proper installation:

  • Incorrect refrigerant charging reduces efficiency by 20% or more
  • Improper airflow setup causes uneven temperatures and premature wear
  • Poor electrical connections create safety hazards and reliability issues
  • Inadequate sealing leads to energy waste and comfort problems

Common DIY Installation Problems

Attempting HVAC installation without professional expertise often results in:

  • Undersized or oversized equipment selection
  • Improper refrigerant handling and environmental violations
  • Code compliance failures and permit issues
  • Voided equipment warranties and insurance problems
  • Safety hazards from gas and electrical work
